1 Introduction

This chapter serves as an introduction to the package ProgressBar.

1.1 Example

The progress bar refreshes the line in the terminal after each iteration. Before the start of an iteration process we call StartProgress, then after each iteration step we call PrintProgress, and after the end of an iteration process we call EndProgress.

gap> LoadPackage("ProgressBar");;
gap> nrIterations := 17;
gap> StartProgress();
gap> for i in [1 .. nrIterations] do
>      Sleep(1);
>      PrintProgress(i, nrIterations);
>    od;
[================================================] 100% | 17/17 | eta 0:00:00
gap> EndProgress();
Terminated after 0:00:19

In the above example we might encounter the following outputs during the execution of the iteration.

[========----------------------------------------]  17% |  3/17 | eta 0:00:17
[===================-----------------------------]  41% |  7/17 | eta 0:00:11
[===============================-----------------]  64% | 11/17 | eta 0:00:06
[==========================================------]  88% | 15/17 | eta 0:00:02
[================================================] 100% | 17/17 | eta 0:00:00
