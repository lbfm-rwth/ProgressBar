  [1X2.2 [33X[0;0YDisplay Options[133X[101X
  
  [33X[0;0YThe methods [11XDeclareProcess[111X, [11XStartProcess[111X and [11XUpdateProcess[111X allow an optional

  the execution of only this single command.[133X
  
  [33X[0;0YFor  modifying  the display output globally for all subsequent executions of

  [33X[0;0YThe following components of [3Xoptrec[103X are supported.[133X

        [33X[0;6Y[9Xfalse[109X to suppress total time. [13XDEFAULT[113X[133X
  
  [8X[10XprintETA[110X[8X[108X
        [33X[0;6Y[9Xtrue[109X to print eta (expected time of arrival/completion). [13XDEFAULT[113X[133X
  
        [33X[0;6Y[9Xfalse[109X to suppress eta.[133X
  
  [8X[10XremoveChildren[110X[8X[108X
        [33X[0;6Y[9Xtrue[109X to remove child process after its termination. [13XDEFAULT[113X[133X

  [8X[10XhighlightCurStep[110X[8X[108X

  [8X[10XhighlightColor[110X[8X[108X

        [33X[0;6Y[9X"blue"[109X for higlighting in blue color.[133X
